Makers of Quality Marshmallows

Riverdale is one of the leading Filipino confectionery manufacturers, started its operation in 2005 as a manufacturing company of marshmallows primarily for exports. Riverdale committed itself to expanding its product line based on the market requirements.

Riverdale is BRC, British Retail Consortium (ISSUE 8) certified, a Global Standard for Food Safety and Conformed by TÜV SÜD Management Service GmbH (043).
