About Us

Riverdale is one of the leading Filipino confectionery manufacturers, started its operation in 2005 as a manufacturing company of marshmallows primarily for exports. Riverdale committed itself to expanding its product line based on the market requirements.

Riverdale is BRC, British Retail Consortium (ISSUE 8) certified, a Global Standard for Food Safety and Conformed by TÜV SÜD Management Service GmbH (043). Furthermore, the company is GMP certified by Food & Drug Administration (Philippines) and ISO 22000 aligned, a manifestation of the company’s thrust to employ globally accepted food manufacturing processes. The company is also a certified Halal food manufacturer.


Our company has the capacity to supply the international markets like Japan, UK and Taiwan.


The flavors and colors are carefully selected and created to satisfy the discriminating taste of the consumers. Our marshmallows are great for snack, roasting, as topping to your favorite dessert, or an additional color and texture for homemade goodies.


Our marshmallows likewise fill the needs of manufacturers of ice cream cakes, and other baked products. Its stable form, vibrant colors, smooth texture, and right sweetness complement the goods offered by large-scale food manufacturers.

Riverdale Confectionery Industry, Inc. is committed to provide a safe working environment, upgrading of skills and capabilities of all its human resources.

  • Implementation of Food Safety and Quality Management System (FSQMS) including Good Manufacturing Practices and HACCP. Periodic review, continual improvement and updating of existing policies and procedures to ensure its effectiveness and suitability.

  • Communication of Food Safety and Quality Policy and company objectives throughout the entire organization.

  • Periodic review of operations - processes, materials, equipment, resources - in all departments to attain optimum operational costs.

  • Introduce innovative products, provide outstanding technical support and service to ensure customer satisfaction.

  • Implement an environmental management system to conserve natural resources and minimize all kinds of wastes.


  • Provision of necessary venue, training and financial resources  to improve competency of all its human resources.
